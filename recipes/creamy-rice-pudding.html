Creamy Rice Pudding

An old fashioned rice pudding that is so creamy with a perfect texture and sweetness. You are going to love this recipe, it is so easy to make and only requires a handful of ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Egg
  • Raisins
  • Butter
  • Vanilla

Steps

  1. Cook the rice.
  2. Cook the cooked rice, some of the milk, sugar, and salt over medium heat until thick.
  3. Stir the remaining milk, egg, and raisins.
  4. Cook for two more minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in the butter and vanilla
